## Tips for Accuracy

1. **Be consistent** - Always use the same portion size for similar foods
2. **Start medium** - When unsure, choose "medium" or "1 serving"
3. **Adjust over time** - If you find you're consistently over/under, adjust your typical portions
4. **Use grams when available** - Kitchen scale at home? Use exact measurements
5. **Round up** - Better to overestimate slightly than underestimate
